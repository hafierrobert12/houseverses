⸻   his story   ⸻

Born on the twelfth of September, 2005 — in a small village tucked into the folds of Dairi district, North Sumatra — Hafier grew up where the forest meets silence, and silence meets words.

Now a student of Library and Information Science at Universitas Sumatera Utara, class of 2023, he built House of Verses as a sanctuary — a place where literature breathes, where every verse finds the reader it was always meant for.

He believes a library is not a building. It is a state of mind.
